Ladakh Expedition

A historic journey from Delhi to Ladakh led by Divyangjan.

The Expedition

In 2022, WCIA along with DCCI organized an expedition from Delhi to Ladakh, covering over 1000 km. The journey was a testament to the spirit of resilience and determination, showcasing that disability is not a barrier to adventure.

The expedition received widespread media coverage and inspired many.

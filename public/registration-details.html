Registration Details

Everything you need to know about registering with WCIA.

  • Registration is open to all persons with disabilities who wish to play wheelchair cricket.
  • Volunteers and coaches are also welcome to register.
  • Fill the online registration form with accurate details.
  • After submission, you will receive a confirmation email with further instructions.
  • Annual membership renewal is required to stay active.
  • For any queries, contact us at office@wcia.tv

About Sewa Niketan

Sewa Niketan is a dedicated rehabilitation center under WCIA, providing physiotherapy, psychological counseling, and vocational training for persons with disabilities. Our aim is to restore confidence and independence.

Located in Lucknow, the center serves as a hub for holistic development, offering sports training, assistive devices, and community integration programs.

#NoticeKaroIndia

A social media movement to highlight the achievements of persons with disabilities.

The Campaign

#NoticeKaroIndia is a digital campaign encouraging people to share stories, photos, and videos of disabled individuals breaking stereotypes. The campaign went viral and helped change perceptions about ability.

Join the movement by posting on social media with the hashtag #NoticeKaroIndia and tagging @wcia.official.

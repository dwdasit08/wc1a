About WCIA

Wheelchair Cricket India Association – a movement that transcends boundaries.

Our Mission

To empower persons with disabilities through the sport of cricket, promoting inclusion, resilience, and national pride. We strive to create a platform where ability is defined by spirit, not by physical limitations.

WCIA is an 80G-approved non-profit organization registered under the Societies Registration Act, 1860. We have been instrumental in forming the Indian Wheelchair Cricket Team and have organized multiple national and international tournaments.

  • Active in 20+ states
  • 1000+ registered players
  • 5 National Championships
  • International Friendship Cup winners

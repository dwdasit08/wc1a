Drive With Ability

A campaign to promote accessible mobility and sports for persons with disabilities.

About the Campaign

Drive With Ability aims to raise awareness about the importance of accessible transportation and sports infrastructure for people with disabilities. Through cricket and other adaptive sports, we empower individuals to break barriers.

This campaign is supported by various organizations and has been instrumental in distributing sports wheelchairs and organizing mobility workshops across India.
